Robotics Day at Corvallis Library

PHRED Team 847 partnered with Crescent Valley High School and local FLL teams for Robotics Day at the Corvallis-Benton County Public Library.
Community members got hands-on experience driving our robots and learning about robotics competitions.

Hosting FTC Competitions

Oregon Spark Invitational (Feb 2025) - Philomath High School hosted this FIRST Tech Challenge tournament, bringing together teams from the area.
These events are free and open to the public, allowing community members to experience robotics firsthand. Read more

Working Together

Six Teams, One Community - PHRED operates FRC Team 847, FTC Teams 8892/24195/27416, and two FLL teams.
We collaborate with teams across Oregon and Washington, sharing knowledge and supporting each other through Coopertition and Gracious Professionalism.
