  • PHRED is a set of teams based in Philomath, Oregon, comprised of elementary, middle, and high school students who participate in the FIRST Robotics program.
Grades 4th - 6th
FLL teams design, build, and program LEGO robots to complete in themed challenges while developing problem-solving, teamwork, and innovation skills.
Grades 6th - 8th
FTC teams design, build, and program mid-sized robots using a modular robotics platform to compete in head-to-head challenges while developing engineering, coding, and teamwork skills.
Grades 8th - 12th
FTC teams design, build, and program large-scale robots in a high-intensity, real-world engineering environment, competing in complex challenges that emphasize innovation, collaboration, and STEM leadership.
